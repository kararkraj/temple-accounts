Welcome to TA

The temple accounts is an app where temple executives can manage donations received by their temple.

They can make entry for every donation and keep track of them.

They can also generate receipts in PDF format and share the same with donation givers.

Step 1 - Registration:

  1. Register to the application by navigating to register page.
  2. After registering, you will receive a verification email.
  3. Click on the link in the email to verify your email Id.
  4. Login with your credentials.

Step 2 - Add Temple and Services:

  1. When you login for the first time, you will be redirected to add temple page.
  2. Add your temple by filling up the temple details.

    3. At least one temple needs to be added to proceed.

  3. Add frequently received donation amount as services.

Final step:

Navigate to add entry page and start entering donation details.

On submit, PDF receipt will be downloaded and opened by default PDF opener.
